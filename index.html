Coconut Modak:

The sweet filling on the inside of a modak consists of freshly grated coconut and jaggery while the outer soft shell is made from rice flour or wheat flour mixed with khava or maida flour.

Potato wedges

Potato wedges are wedges of potatoes, often large and unpeeled, that are either baked or fried. It can served with sour cream and sweet chilli, ketchup, ranch, and honey mustard. They are usually seasoned with a variety of spices, commonly paprika, salt and pepper.

Chocolate chip muffins

Chocolate chip muffins are the perfect breakfast or after dinner treat. Sweet and loaded with rich chocolate chips, these muffins are great just out of the oven or eaten at room temperature.

Tandoori chicken

Tandoori chicken is chicken dish prepared by roasting chicken marinated in yoghurt and spices in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven. The dish originated from the Indian subcontinent and is popular in many other parts of the world.Tandoori chicken can be eaten as a starter or appetizer, or as a main course, often served with naan flatbread. It is also used as the base of numerous cream-based curries, such as butter chicken.

Chicken Curry

Punjabi Chicken curry is a classic Indian dish; vibrant with rich flavorful gravy. It is a popular Dhaba style dish with succulent chicken pieces cooked in a heartwarming spice mix. The rich creamy gravy is what makes this Punjabi Chicken curry exotic and appealing. With basic ingredients and Indian spices, this restaurant style Punjabi Chicken curry can be made easily at home.
